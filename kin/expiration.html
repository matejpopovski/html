Software Expiration

Is some of your software near or past its expiration date? Software isn't good for as long as it used to be. Security updates need to be applied regularly, and older software must be upgraded to continue receiving patches. Please check the table below and contact us for assistance.

We're all busy, and patches and upgrades can easily be overlooked. Square Circle Consulting offers remote patch management, a service which improves security, performance and reliability by patching the most vulnerable software on a regular basis. It also includes checks for over 100 computer health issues such as disk errors, faulty RAM and backup functionality. Please contact us to schedule.
